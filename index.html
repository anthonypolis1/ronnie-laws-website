Biography

Ronnie Laws is a legendary jazz, blues, and funk saxophonist who has captivated audiences for over five decades. Born in Houston, Texas, Laws developed his distinctive sound that blends smooth jazz with R&B and funk influences.

Throughout his illustrious career, Laws has released numerous critically acclaimed albums and collaborated with some of the biggest names in music, including Earth, Wind & Fire, Quincy Jones, and Stevie Wonder.

His signature smooth saxophone style has influenced generations of musicians and continues to inspire jazz lovers around the world.
